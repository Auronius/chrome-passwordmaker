    Information

    Keyboard navigation
    You can use the shortcut Alt+Z to open the PasswordMaker Pro popup, no matter where you are on a website.

    When you are done typing your master password in the Password textfield of the PassworkMaker Pro popup, you can push Enter to fill the open website'spassword field with the generated password, using the settings that were preselected in the PasswordMaker Pro popup.

    If you do not have the Keep Master Password Hash setting enabled, pushing Enter or Tab in the Password textfield will bring you to the Confirmation textfield. Here you can then push Enter, after you are done typing your master password again, to fill the open website's password field, using the settings that were preselected in the password popup.

    If you want to alter any of the PasswordMaker Pro popup's settings, just push Tab to get to the respective next setting. Push Shift+Tab to get to the previous setting respectively. When you are in the Generated Password field, again you can push Enter to fill the open website's password field.
